Account types:
- Patient: can view his own medical record, and choose to discharge himself
- Doctor: can view all the patients, can treat any patient, can view the medical record for any patient, can view his own treatment record
- Administrator: can update the accounts for patients, doctors and other admins; can edit facilities stuff
